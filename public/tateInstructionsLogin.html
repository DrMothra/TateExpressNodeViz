Login

This is where you sign in to your account using your username and password. Your username is used to denote who the author of each graph is and will be displayed to everyone that uses the app (so you may want to be careful what you enter!?).

If you don't have an account yet then just click the Create Account button and enter your details.

If you enter an incorrect username then you will be informed to either re-type them or create and account.
